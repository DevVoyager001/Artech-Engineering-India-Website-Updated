About Us

Artech Engineering India, formerly known as Suraj Construction, has been a pioneering force in the realm of Steel Structural fabrication & erection, Piping, Project & Mechanical Maintenance work, civil jobs, and COMMANDO Manpower supply since its inception in 1985. Over the years, our commitment to quality, safety, and client satisfaction has propelled us to new heights.

Beginning our journey with Ispat Profiles Pune in 1985, we later shifted our focus to Ispat Industries Ltd., Dolvi, in 1993. In 2010, we rebranded ourselves as Artech Engineering India, marking a new chapter in our legacy of excellence.

With a track record of successful collaborations with industry leaders such as M/s Ispat Profiles India Ltd., M/s. Nippon Denro Ispat Ltd., M/s. JSW Steel Ltd., and many more, we take pride in our role as a reliable partner in the growth and success of our clients.

Our journey is marked by a commitment to safety, quality control, and the deployment of a skilled workforce. Artech Engineering India stands as a symbol of innovation, reliability, and expertise in the steel, power, and chemical industries.

Choose Artech Engineering India for your projects, and experience the assurance of unparalleled service and excellence that spans over three decades.
